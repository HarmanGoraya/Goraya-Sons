Goraya & Sons, Inc

We are a small, family-owned business that has been operating for over 5 years. We specialize in the transportation of goods across the country,
with a focus on delivering time-sensitive and temperature-controlled cargo.

Visit us to find out more

Services

Our flatbed division offers a full range of services. We specialize in less-than-truckload (LTL) freight and brokerage.

Equipment

When you call us with freight that needs to be delicately handled, we have the solution. We offer pad, E-bar, decking, and ramp services. Our equipment protects your freight and reduce your damage claims. Reducing claims saves you needless expense.

Full Truckload

FTL streamlines the logistics of the transportation process. Additionally, the FTL transport goods faster as they do not have to deliver the items to various customers. It also decreases the chances of the items being damaged due to constant reloading.

Reefer Trucks

Fruits, vegetables, cheese, poultry, and fish are transported via refrigerated trucks. Several pharmaceutical companies also use refrigerated trucks to transport medicines and chemicals.
